Mold and Die Tooling Design, Fabrication, and Repair Services

B&J Specialty, Inc. provides complete mold and die design, fabrication, and repair services. Whether it is the repair or modification of existing tooling, or complete fabrication of a new mold or die set, our in-house team has the tools and capabilities to complet any job. Examples of mold fabrication capabilities include plastic injection, shuttle, single & multi-cavity, stack/tandem, and collapsible cores. See the box below for more examples of our mold fabrication capabilities. Die types we manufacture include transfer, progressive, single hit, and blanking dies.


All tooling is fabricated from the highest quality tool steel, copper, beryllium copper, or aluminum alloy, in dimensions up to 78" X 62" X 72". Our equipment is capable of machining components up to 15,000 lbs., lifting capacity of 30,000 lbs., and transportation capacity of 55,000 lbs., with typical lead times of only 6-8 weeks on new tooling. We understand that information is key. Our customers are issued weekly progress reports that provide details of a project's progress and delivery schedule.


At B&J Specialty, Inc., we understand the manufacturing environment. When a critical piece of tooling breaks, customers do not have months to wait on repairs. Our 24-hour emergency mold and die repair services get customers up and running with minimal downtime, while our comprehensive preventative maintenance plans help prevent downtime altogether. In-house pick-up and delivery offers customers the highest degree of convenience and speed.


All work is done to customer specified or industry standard tolerance, with a standard dimensional tolerance of ±.0005. CMM equipment is capable of measuring parts up to 39.5" X 63" X 24", and optical comparators can match parts directly with CAD drawings. We are a family-owned company with a record of continual growth over our 40+ years in business. This growth is a result of our extensive range of capabilities, expedient lead times, and dedication to our customers.
